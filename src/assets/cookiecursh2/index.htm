How to Play

You have to combine at least three delicious cookies to make them vanish from the field. If you manage to combine even more cookies at the same time, you will be rewarded with tons of points and of course with cool extras that can for instance wipe a whole line of sweets or make all cookies of a certain color vanish. Don't rush your moves and always keep an eye on your score as the turn limit is challenging and somtimes you need to plan a few moves ahead. Play Cookie Crush now for free and enjoy the cookies.
